Drawscapes is a EPSRC funded research project carried by Eduardo Rico in the Bartlett School of Architecture. The proposed project for UCL EAST is a theoretical exercise with the aim of learning about processes of online collaborative design. Public participation and consultation for UCL EAST project has already taken place and their results can be consulted in www.ucl.ac.uk/ucl-east
